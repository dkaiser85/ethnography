Introduction

Welcome! Below is the structure for conducting interviews based on survey results. Select a category and score to view tailored questions.

Introduction Script:
Hello, thank you for participating in this interview. My name is Ben Ortlip, and I am the founder of The Culture MRI®. Your responses will remain confidential and anonymous. This conversation aims to validate survey results and deepen understanding of your experiences.

Warm-Up Questions:

  • Can you describe a typical day at work for you?
  • How has your experience been working at this company so far?
  • What are some of the things you enjoy most about your job, and what are some of the challenges you face?
